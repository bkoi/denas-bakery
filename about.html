Our Story

About eight years ago I had to accept, sadly, that I was no longer able to eat whatever I wanted to, because I had developed gluten sensitivity and lactose intolerance. Having been an avid baker since my childhood, it totally baffled me how difficult it was to make springy bread, gorgeous, light-weight sponge cakes or a nice, chewy pizza without relying on the gluten found in most types of flour. As my initial efforts did not pay off and all my creations turned out to be too dense in texture, I got disheartened and gave up baking. Only for a while. When I met up a friend for a catch-up, she took me to a lovely caf√© where I tasted my first proper, gluten free lemon polenta cake which was surprisingly light in texture and burst with flavour. After a nice chat with the waitress, I managed to get the recipe, and the rest, as they say, is history. Since then, I have researched and perfected the gluten free recipes that we use in our bakery, because I genuinely believe that eating is a source of enjoyment, and even those with a gluten-related eating disorder have the right to it.


Enjoy our creations and please feel free to contact us if you would like us to add your favourite gluten free recipe to our menu for everyone to enjoy!
